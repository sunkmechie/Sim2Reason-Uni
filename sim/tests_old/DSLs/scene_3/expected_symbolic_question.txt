Both hanging blocks (m_3 kg and m_4 kg on left and right side respectively) are suspended vertically while initally maintaining contact with their respective side edges of the box.
What is the magnitude of the acceleration of the block on top of the box in the system 'mass_box_plane' after t seconds?
