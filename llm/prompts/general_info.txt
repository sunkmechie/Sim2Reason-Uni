Assume acceleration due to gravity as 9.81 m/s^2, all strings inextensible, and all surfaces frictionless unless stated otherwise. All bodies are initially at rest unless stated otherwise. For inclined-plane scenes, unless otherwise stated, the along-plane direction is the plane local +x axis (left to right), the transverse in-plane direction is local +y, and positive plane inclination is counterclockwise with respect to the viewing axis. Directional words such as left/right/up/down use that same viewer perspective.
